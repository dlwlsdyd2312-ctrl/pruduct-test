Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026

Effective: January 1, 2026

Welcome to LottoPro ("we", "our", or "us"). We operate the website pruduct-test.pages.dev (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website.

By using LottoPro, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

1. Information We Collect

LottoPro is a client-side, browser-based tool. We do not require registration or collect any personally identifiable information directly. We do not have databases storing user profiles, generated numbers, or personal details.

The limited data that may be collected includes:

  • Usage data via Google Analytics — anonymized data such as pages visited, time on site, browser type, device type, and country. This data does not identify you personally.
  • Local storage data — your theme preference (light/dark mode) is stored in your browser's localStorage. This stays on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.
  • Contact form submissions — if you use our contact form, your name, email address, and message are sent directly to our form service provider (Formspree). See Section 5 for details.

2. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors interact with our website. Google Analytics collects anonymized data including:

  • Pages viewed and time spent on each page
  • Referring websites and search terms
  • Browser, device type, and operating system
  • Approximate geographic location (country/city level)

This data is aggregated and cannot be used to identify individual users. Google's data practices are governed by the Google Privacy Policy. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

3. Google AdSense and Advertising

LottoPro displays advertisements provided by Google AdSense. Google uses cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website and other websites on the internet.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and other sites on the Internet. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

The following types of data may be used for advertising purposes by Google:

  • Cookie identifiers and browsing behavior
  • Device and browser information
  • IP address (used to infer general location)
  • Interaction with ads (clicks, impressions)

LottoPro does not control the specific advertisements shown and does not receive personal data from Google in connection with advertising. For more information, visit Google's Advertising Policies.

4. Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. LottoPro uses the following types of cookies:

  • Strictly necessary cookies — required for the website to function (e.g., remembering your theme preference via localStorage).
  • Analytics cookies — placed by Google Analytics to understand website usage.
  • Advertising cookies — placed by Google AdSense to deliver relevant advertisements.

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website. For more information about managing cookies, visit AllAboutCookies.org.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google Analytics — website analytics. Privacy Policy
  • Google AdSense — advertising. Privacy Policy
  • Formspree — contact form processing. Your name, email, and message are transmitted to Formspree servers when you submit our contact form. Formspree Privacy Policy
  • Google Fonts — typography. Google may collect limited usage data. Privacy Policy
  • Cloudflare Pages — website hosting. Cloudflare may log access requests. Privacy Policy

6. Data Retention

Since LottoPro does not collect personal data directly, we do not retain personal data on our servers. Analytics data retained by Google Analytics is subject to Google's own data retention policies (default: 14 months). Contact form data submitted via Formspree is retained per Formspree's policies.

7. Children's Privacy

LottoPro is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Lottery participation is restricted to adults in most jurisdictions. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information through our service, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • The right to access information about data collected about you
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to opt out of data collection for advertising purposes
  • Rights under GDPR (European Union), CCPA (California), and other applicable laws

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us via the form on our website.

9. Links to Other Websites

Our website contains links to external sites, including official lottery websites and result pages. These external sites have their own privacy policies, and we are not responsible for their content or privacy practices. We encourage you to review the privacy policy of any external site you visit.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We will indicate the date of the most recent update at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please reach out to us through the contact form on our main website. We will do our best to respond within 48 hours.
